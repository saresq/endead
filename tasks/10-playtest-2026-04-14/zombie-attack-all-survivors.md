## Rules Reference
Per Zombicide 2E rules: During the Zombie Attack step, **each Zombie in a zone with Survivors attacks** (auto-hit, 1 Wound each). **Players choose how to distribute Wounds** among Survivors in the zone.

So if 4 zombies and 2 survivors are in a zone, there are 4 Wounds total, and players decide how to split them (e.g., 2 each, or 3+1, etc.).

## What to Check
- The current behavior where zombies attack ALL players is actually **correct per the rules**
- However, verify that players can choose how to distribute the wounds
- If wound distribution choice is not implemented, add it (UI prompt for player to assign wounds)
